SIGTYP

This is the website of the ACL Special Interest Group on Typology (SIGTYP)

The purpose of SIGTYP is to promote the study of linguistic typology in the ACL community and to provide members of the ACL with a means of exchanging news of recent research developments in linguistic typology and multilingual NLP. SIGTYP organizes an annual workshop collocated with a *ACL conference. We also organize a lecture series and newsletter that showcase research from the community.
